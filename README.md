# WhoPaysWhatPrivacyPolicy
Privacy Policy

WhoPaysWhat does not collect, store, or share personal data.

All data entered into the app, including people, items, and receipt information, exists only on the user’s device. WhoPaysWhat does not require an account and does not transmit data to external servers.

Camera access is used solely for scanning receipts at the user’s request. Images are processed locally on the device and are not stored or shared by the app.

WhoPaysWhat does not use third-party analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising services.
